Industrial Revolution

The industrial revolution was a time when machines started to take over the work that humans used to do. And forever changed the way industry worked.

First Steam Engine

The first steam engine was invented by Thomas Newcomen in the 1712. This was very important because it turnd into things like cars, planes, and tanks. And made factorys in the industrial revolution more efficient.

Middle Ages

For the middle ages I will be talking about trebuchets and their impacts on the middle ages. Trebuchets were a type of catapult that used a counterweight to launch a payload instead of tension like traditional catapults. They were invented in the 11th century and were used to launch heavy rocks over half a mile into a castle wall.
